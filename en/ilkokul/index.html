PRIMARY SCHOOL

Gazi College Primary School

We provide contemporary education at grades 1-4. With a total capacity of 120 students in 8 branches, we give special attention to each student.

In the first 4 years where the foundations of academic development are laid, our branch teachers actively support the education process. In addition to teaching literacy, we implement special programs that develop creative thinking and communication skills.

With our modern cafeteria, playground, and gymnasium, we provide our students with a safe and enjoyable learning environment. Our garden, integrated with natural life, supports our students' social and emotional development.
