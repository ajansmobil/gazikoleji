MIDDLE SCHOOL

Gazi College Middle School

We offer a comprehensive education program aimed at academic excellence for our 5th-8th grade students.

Our intensive English program prepares our students for the global world. With our rational disciplinary understanding and modern educational approach, we aim to help our students realize their potential at the highest level.

We implement special communication and development programs for our adolescent students. We support scientific thinking, academic success, and social development in a balanced way. With our regular assessment and evaluation system, we closely monitor our students' development and provide one-on-one support in areas where they need it.
