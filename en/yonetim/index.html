Management

Dear Parents, Dear Students,

Since its establishment, we have worked to create a school culture at Gazi College, with the ideal of "education for life" at the center of our school culture. We work to prepare our students not only for exams but for all areas of life and social living. Our Education programs, designed in line with the basic principles of National Education and the path of contemporary civilization drawn by Atatürk, are implemented with great dedication by our expert education administrators and teachers.

As of the 2018-2019 Academic Year, our educational framework, starting from Kindergarten, has been completed with our Anatolian and Science High Schools. Gazi College, designed for approximately six hundred students from age four to sixteen in two separate campuses, serves our city, our country, and bright tomorrows.

I would like to thank our parents who have made great contributions to the establishment and development of our schools and who have entrusted their most valuable assets, their children, to us from day one, and to my students who have always been a source of happiness with their presence.

May success and happiness always be with you and us.

Savaş BEKTAŞOĞLU - FOUNDER
