PRESCHOOL
At Gazi College Kindergarten, special education is provided to our students from the age of 4. Boutique education is provided with a small number of students in each class.
PRIMARY SCHOOL
At Gazi College Primary School, education is provided at grades 1-4. We contribute to our students' academic and social development with our expert teachers.
MIDDLE SCHOOL
With the intensified English program in Gazi College Secondary School, a training aimed at high academic success is offered.
STUDY
In order to increase the academic success of our students, one -on -one study programs are applied by our expert teachers.
Get to Know Gazi College
Founded in 2015 by Expert Teacher and Education Manager Savaş BEKTAŞOĞLU. The school accepted its first students in the 2016-2017 academic year. Bektaşoğlu, who is always proud of being a teacher's child and is himself a Turkish Language and Literature Teacher, emphasizes that he sees Education as a tool to create an ideal Turkey and a world full of peace and tranquility, and states that their most important goal is to raise happy children who will contribute to the homeland and the whole world.
Our Vision
To be a contemporary and enlightened educational institution where students are educated according to their individual characteristics, and to raise happy, peaceful, hardworking, planned, and moral generations in this institution.
Our Mission
To raise a generation that respects universal values, is raised with local cultural elements, is aware of different cultures, uses their native language and body language very well, has strong communication skills, masters science and arts, and possesses principles of contemporary civilization.
