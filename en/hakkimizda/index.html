About Us

GAZI COLLEGE is an established and innovative educational institution that has been operating in the education sector since 2015. Founded under the visionary leadership of Expert Teacher and Education Manager Savaş BEKTAŞOĞLU, our school opened its doors to students in the 2016-2017 academic year. Our founder, Savaş BEKTAŞOĞLU, sees education as the cornerstone of social development and global peace, aiming to raise the leaders of the future.

Our campus, reflecting a modern educational approach, consists of two carefully designed buildings in a prestigious location in Gaziantep. Our campus is equipped with playgrounds, sports facilities, and social activity centers specially arranged for each age group, providing education for a wide age range from 4 years old to 8th grade. Our institution aims for excellence in education with its unique educational program developed by expert educators and "All-Inclusive Concept".
