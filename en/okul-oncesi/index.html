Gazi College Kindergarten

We provide special education opportunities for our children from the age of 4. We embrace the boutique education approach with a total capacity of 40 students in 3 branches.

We help children develop motor skills, regular study habits, and superior communication abilities at an early age. Our English education is supported by sports, art, dance, music, and painting lessons. We provide quality education with our expert teachers and support staff.

Our kindergarten section serves in a separate area equipped with special hygiene standards. Our cafeteria, playgrounds, and gymnasium are specially designed for our children's age groups. We offer interaction opportunities with pets and plants in our nature-integrated garden. We maintain our personalized education approach by accepting students with limited quotas each year.
